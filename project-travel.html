Travel Booking Platform

Comprehensive travel booking system with flight, hotel, and activity reservations

A full-service travel platform that aggregates flights, hotels, and activities from multiple providers. Features intelligent price comparison, personalized recommendations, and seamless booking experience with integrated payment processing and travel management tools.

Project Highlights

API Integration

Integrated with major travel APIs including Amadeus, Booking.com, and Expedia. Built custom aggregation layer to compare prices across 50+ airlines and 100,000+ hotels worldwide in real-time.

Smart Recommendations

Implemented ML-powered recommendation engine using user preferences, booking history, and seasonal trends. Achieved 40% increase in conversion rates through personalized travel suggestions.

Business Growth

Platform processes over 1,000 bookings monthly with $2M+ in transaction volume. Reduced booking abandonment by 35% through streamlined checkout process and mobile optimization.
